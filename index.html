Australia Weather Temperature Analysis

Author: Julien Siew Wilson

This data comes from the Australian Bureau of Meteorology (BoM) and the Australian Climate Service (ACS)

Top stations by annual mean temperature cluster in Australia’s north and interior; values are tightly packed, suggesting small differences at the extreme warm end.
Locations with the highest average maximums are largely NT/WA outposts (e.g., Katherine/Darwin region), consistent with tropical and semi-arid climates.
Sunshine vs Max Temperature: gentle positive relationship — more sunshine generally accompanies higher max temperatures up to ~35 °C; variability increases in mid-ranges across cities.
Rainfall vs Max Temperature: heavier rain events appear at moderate temperatures; the hottest days tend to have lower rainfall, hinting at drier hot conditions.
Mean temperature map: warmer stations concentrate in the Top End and inland areas; cooler means appear in the south and higher-latitude coastal regions.
